Wardruna Shares New Single And Video For “Voluspá”, New Album Incoming

Wardruna has shared an incredible video for their new single, ‘Voluspá’. Based on an old Norse poem (Prophecy of the Seeress), the video was created with Finnish directors Finnish film, all led by Tuukka Koski and Koski Syväri. You can purchase and stream the single today on all platforms and watch the clip below. New album Skal coming soon.

Thom Yorke Shares New Mix for BBC Radio 3: Listen

Thom Yorke has shared a new mix of music, recorded for BBC Radio 3. The wide-ranging mix includes music from krautrock pioneers Faust, Aphex Twin, composers like Pierre Henry and Karlheinz Stockhausen, and many others. It also features Radiohead’s single “Burn the Witch” (from A Moon Shaped Pool) and Yorke’s recent song “Suspirium” for the Suspiria soundtrack. Take a listen to the full mix here.

Last month, Yorke sat down with Mary Anne Hobbs on BBC Radio 6 to talk about his Suspiria soundtrack and shared an hour-long mix there as well. In another interview, he also discussed his next solo full-length, describing it as “political.”

Eagles of Death Metal Announce New Vinyl-Only Covers Album

Eagles of Death Metal have announced a new album of covers. Pigeons of Shit Metal—a title that references an old Axl Rose diss—is available only on vinyl. The 10-track album features covers of songs by the Ramones, the Pixies, Love and Rockets, AC/CD, the Distillers, Queens of the Stone Age, and of course, Guns N’ Roses. The limited edition vinyl features artwork by Josh Homme and is available in colors such as glow-in-the dark, neon pink, and cyan blue. Pre-order Pigeons of Shit Metal and see the full tracklist via Last Hurrah Records.

Eagles of Death Metal’s last original LP was 2015’s Zipper Down.
